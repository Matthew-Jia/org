# Reasons for writing notes

Here are all the current reasosn why I write notes:
- \textbf{Writing out my thoughts}: Writing notes helps me formalize the junk of thoughts in my brain into full, polished thoughts. This helps me realize gaps in my thinking and also forces me to rethink about the information over and over again until it makes sense on paper, which helps my brain retain the details.
- \textbf{Notes to reference}: Writing out notes means that you can reference your notes later when you inevitably forget something.
- \textbf{Practice}: Helps me practice md/latex and neovim movements.

# Rules for Notes

- Notes are all stored inside of the ~/notes folder. Do not organize files until it is obvious that cetain files should be group together.
- When hyperlinking between files, make sure sections are linked, at the bare minimum, explicitly from the notes base folder. This will make moving topics to different folder easier, since we can just find and replace all the occurences of the hyperlinks from their previous file in the notes directory to their new file.
